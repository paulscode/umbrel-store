Electrs Liquid

A Liquid full node and Electrum indexer, running on your Umbrel.

Connect a wallet on your network

Point an Electrum-compatible Liquid wallet at this server:

  • Host: your Umbrel's address (e.g. umbrel.local or its LAN IP)
  • Port: 50101 (plaintext TCP)

Use it from another Umbrel app

Add paulscode-electrs-liquid as a dependency and read APP_ELECTRS_LIQUID_ELECTRS_NODE_IP and APP_ELECTRS_LIQUID_ELECTRS_NODE_PORT (internal Electrum port 50001).

First sync

The Liquid chain and its address index are large (~115 GB of disk) and the first sync can take many hours. The endpoint becomes usable once both the Liquid node and the Electrum index have finished syncing — watch the app's logs for progress.
